Chicken recipie

Description

This is a nice ckicken recipie. It is so tasty, it can make an angry man smile!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Frying oil
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. You get yourself a nice frying pan
  2. You cut your chicken into a dish
  3. You then fry the chicken till they are brown
  4. You add seasonings of your choice
  5. Bon appetite!
